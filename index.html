Yves Saint Laurent (YSL)

The name Yves Saint Laurent (YSL) epitomizes cutting-edge Parisian fashion and exemplifies an approach to creating your own rules for destructive, seductive and dreamy beauty. YSL Beauty revitalizes the mind and soul through makeup, fragrance and skincare. Bold lip colors, rock 'n' roll eyes, and impeccable radiance – all combine timeless luxury with exhilarating modernity.

Yves Saint Laurent (YSL) makeup expresses fashion's hottest trends and lasting impressions with radiant skin and rich colors. Instantly wake up your skin with the perfect complexion lineup of Yves Saint Laurent foundations, concealers, primers, and the best-selling Radiance Perfecting Pen. Create rock-chic eyes with drama-boosting mascara for edgy lashes and eye-catching couture eyeshadow. Drenched lips in rich hues with Yves Saint Laurent (YSL) Lipstick. Our current move? Rouge Volupte Shine, a stick oil lipstick that delivers high-performance color, capricious shine, intense hydration and antioxidant care.

Yves Saint Laurent (YSL) beauty goes beyond looks. Capture the feeling. Yves Saint Laurent (YSL) fragrances for women exude femininity and beauty with iconic scents such as Black Opium, a thrilling coffee floral scent, and Opium, a captivating blend of lush florals, rich spice, and deep wood notes. Embodies sensuality. The Yves Saint Laurent (YSL) men's cologne collection is equally alluring with a completely unique lineup of fragrances just for him. L'Homme Ultime celebrates masculinity and freedom with a bold blend of sensual sage and woody notes, while La Nuit De L'Homme tells a story of strength and mystery with the contrasting powers of cedar and cardamom.

Synonymous with sexy and subversive beauty, Yves Saint Laurent (YSL) skincare, makeup and fragrances offer a revolutionary experience to the wearer. Discover these signature colors and scents when shopping online at Sephora.
